# NPC Labs: Scaling the Web3 Creator Economy

## The Challenge
Creators face immense challenges in securing and monetizing their intellectual property (IP) globally. Traditional systems lack transparent provenance and often fail to bridge the gap between digital ownership and physical fulfillment.

## Architectural Deep-Dive
### On-Chain Provenance & Verification
I architected the product vision to ensure every creative order is inscribed on-chain. This establishes an immutable cryptographic record, protecting creators from IP theft and providing customers with verifiable authenticity.

### Digital-to-Physical Bridge
One of the most complex aspects was connecting on-chain verification with a global logistics and fulfillment network. I integrated the platform with physical-world suppliers used by Amazon and Tesla, ensuring high-quality, sustainable (100% recyclable) delivery at scale.

## Business Impact
Under my leadership as Head of Product, NPC Labs has secured over 10,000 digital assets and serves an active community of 5 million users, successfully scaling a decentralized ecosystem that bridges digital IP with physical reality.
